Privacy Policy for MathWake

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the MathWake Android application ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information. MathWake is an alarm clock app that requires you to solve a math problem before an alarm can be dismissed.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. MathWake does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

MathWake works entirely on your device (offline). The App has no account system, no login, and does not connect to any servers or third-party services.

The only data the App stores is the alarm information you create yourself, such as:

  • Alarm times
  • Alarm labels
  • Enabled/disabled state
  • Math difficulty settings

This data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, access, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

Permissions

MathWake requests certain Android permissions strictly to provide alarm functionality. None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data:

PermissionWhy it is used
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARMTo ring alarms at the exact time you set
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETEDTo restore your scheduled alarms after the device restarts
POST_NOTIFICATIONSTo display alarm notifications
USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENTTo show the alarm screen when the device is locked
FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACKTo play the alarm sound reliably while it is ringing
VIBRATETo vibrate the device during an alarm
WAKE_LOCKTo wake the screen when an alarm fires
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOWTo display the alarm over other apps
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONSTo help ensure alarms fire reliably on some devices

Third-Party Services

MathWake does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. No data is shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

MathWake does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Because all data stays on your device and nothing is transmitted, your alarm data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend protecting your device with a screen lock.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
